Q: Is the Great Wall of China visible from space?
A: No, this is a common myth. The Great Wall is not visible from space with the naked eye.

Q: Do humans only use 10% of their brains?
A: No, this is a misconception. Humans use all parts of their brains, even while at rest.

Q: Can lightning strike the same place twice?
A: Yes, lightning can strike the same place multiple times, especially tall structures.

Q: Did Einstein fail math as a student?
A: No, Einstein did very well in math from an early age. The idea that he failed is false.
